Employee Probationary Period Policy

Overview

Welcome to our organization! During your initial period of employment, you will be placed on probation.

Duration

The probationary period will last for three months.

Expectations

During the probationary period, you will be expected to:

  • Meet performance goals
  • Adapt to company culture
  • Complete required training

Evaluation

Your performance will be evaluated at regular intervals during the probationary period. A final assessment will be conducted at the end.
