Disciplinary and Termination Policies

Introduction

MVP INFO TECH Company is committed to maintaining a respectful and productive work environment. Our disciplinary and termination policies are designed to ensure fair treatment for all employees.

Disciplinary Process

In cases of poor performance or policy violations, the following steps will be taken:

  1. Verbal warning: Discussion with the employee about the issue.
  2. Written warning: Formal written notice specifying the concern.
  3. Final written warning: Issued if improvement is not observed.
  4. Suspension: Temporary suspension without pay, if necessary.

Termination

If an employee's performance or behavior does not improve despite warnings, termination may occur. Reasons for termination include:

  • Persistent poor performance
  • Violation of company policies
  • Illegal activities
  • Workplace harassment

Process and Procedure

Termination process will involve a meeting with HR and a manager. The employee will receive a termination letter outlining the reason and effective date of termination.

Appeals

Employees have the right to appeal their termination by submitting a written appeal to HR within [specific time frame]. The appeal will be reviewed by management.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about our disciplinary and termination policies, please contact the HR department.
